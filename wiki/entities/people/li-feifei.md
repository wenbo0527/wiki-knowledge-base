## 关键贡献

### ImageNet
- **计算机视觉最大数据集**
- **深度学习突破的关键**：ImageNet挑战赛推动了AlexNet等突破
- **开源精神**：数据集公开，推动了整个领域的发展

### AI伦理
- **以人为中心的AI**：推动AI伦理和安全研究
- **HAI研究院**：研究AI对社会的影响
- **AI Diversity**：推动AI领域多元化

### 技术研究
- **计算机视觉**：目标检测、图像识别
- **深度学习**：CNN在视觉领域的应用
- **认知视觉**：结合认知科学研究视觉智能

---

## 核心观点

### AI伦理
> "AI is too important to be left to a few tech companies. We need diverse voices in AI development."

> "The goal of AI should be to augment human capabilities, not replace humans."

### AI安全
> "We need to build AI that is robust, fair, and beneficial to humanity."

> "AI safety is not an afterthought. It must be built into the development process from day one."

### AI未来
> "The next wave of AI will be about making AI accessible to everyone, not just tech companies."
