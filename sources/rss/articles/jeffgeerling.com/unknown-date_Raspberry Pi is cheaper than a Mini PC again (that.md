# Raspberry Pi is cheaper than a Mini PC again (that's not good)

> 来源: jeffgeerling.com  
> 发布时间: Mon, 05 Jan 2026 16:00:00 -0600  
> 分类: 海外技术博客  
> 优先级: medium

## 摘要

<p>Almost a year ago, I found that <a href="https://www.jeffgeerling.com/blog/2025/intel-n100-better-value-raspberry-pi/">N100 Mini PCs were cheaper than a decked-out Raspberry Pi 5</a>. So comparing systems with:</p>
<ul>
<li>16GB of RAM</li>
<li>512GB NVMe SSD</li>
<li>Including case, cooler, and power adapter</li>
</ul>
<p>Back in March last year, a GMKtec Mini PC was $159, and a similar-spec Pi 5 was $208.</p>
<p>Today? The <a href="https://amzn.to/4srcKB3">same GMKtec Mini PC</a> is $246.99, and the same Pi 5 is $246.95:</p>
<figure class="insert-image"><img alt="GMKtec N100 Mini PC vs Pi 5 16GB pricing 2025" height="auto" src="https://www.jeffgeerling.com/blog/2026/raspberry-pi-cheaper-than-mini-pc/costs-gmktec-vs-pi-5.jpg" width="700" />
</figure>

<p>Today, because of the wonderful RAM shortages<sup id="fnref:1"><a class="footnote-ref" href="https://www.jeffgeerling.com/blog.xml#fn:1">1</a></sup>, the Mini PC is the same price as a fully kitted-out Raspberry Pi 5.</p>
